Privacy Policy for Walk Alarm

Last updated: 2025-09-18

Walk Alarm ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our alarm app, which motivates you to walk and burn calories before the alarm can be dismissed.

1. Information We Collect

  • Step count & activity data: We access motion sensors or fitness data to measure your steps for dismissing the alarm.
  • Basic app usage data: Non-personal analytics such as crash reports and feature usage (if enabled).
  • No personal identifiers: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or precise location.

2. How We Use Information

  • To verify your steps so you can turn off the alarm.
  • To calculate calories burned (approximate) and encourage healthy habits.
  • To improve app stability and performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share anonymous crash/usage data with trusted service providers (e.g., Google Firebase) solely to improve app performance.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You can revoke sensor permissions (step counter, activity recognition) at any time in your device settings, though this may affect functionality.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, which deletes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
